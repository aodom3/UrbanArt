Krog Street

This is located in East Atlanta on the edge of Cabbagetown and Reynoldstown. It is easily accessible by car and walking. However, depending on the time of day, traffic can be a nightmare - which could be an awesome time to snap a few pics while waiting in traffic. Give a little background history on the place. It is rated highly on both trip advisor and yelp as an awesome free thing to do in Atlanta. There are some pretty dope peices along and around the tunnel. If you dread going through the traffic to get there, check out this gallery below.
